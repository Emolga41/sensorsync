FEATURED SENSORS

Aquarium Acidity S2V

Ever wondered why your pet fishes keep falling sick? It may be due to acidic water but fear not! We have sensors that can monitor the acidity of water in aquariums and provide you with the essential data.

Works for aquariums with different shapes, sizes, water levels and more!

Plant Health Monitor V3

Ever noticed the degrading health of your plants but can't figure out why? Check out the all new Plant Health Monitor which can collect data from your plants by using artificial intelligence and image recognition!

Works for most species of plants and is suitable to be used for miniature gardens.

Survalence Sensor V3A

A common industrial problem is leakage in factory plants. Fear not! We have survalence sensors which will ensure the factory plants remain in great condition by using it's powerful motors.

Targets larger industries and works well with most factory plants, can only be purchased in bulk.
