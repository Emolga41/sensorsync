ABOUT US

About sensorSYNC

sensorSYNC is a company dedicated to developing innovative and reliable sensors for research and development. We are passionate about empowering researchers with the tools they need to make groundbreaking discoveries.



Our products

Our sensors are designed for both industrial and commercial use, offering diverse systems for everyday research. They cater to various mediums like water and soil, providing data on acidity, temperature, and more. The sensors utilize Bluetooth connectivity and come in various sizes and prices, making them accessible to everyone.






Supporting the SDGs

Our remote modular sensor nodes contribute to achieving several Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). By providing real-time data on environmental parameters like water quality, soil conditions, and air pollution, our sensors empower informed decision-making for sustainable resource management and environmental protection. This aligns with SDG 6 (Clean Water and Sanitation), SDG 12 (Responsible Consumption and Production), and SDG 13 (Climate Action).



Empowering People with Data

At sensorSYNC, we believe in the power of data to drive positive change. Our sensors are designed to provide real-time insights into various environments, empowering individuals and organizations to make informed decisions and take action.
